Wildlife Rescue Methods and Techniques

Techniques

  • Search and Rescue Operations: Teams are trained to locate and rescue animals in distress.
  • Medical Assistance: First aid and medical treatment for injured wildlife.
  • Rehabilitation: Caring for rescued animals until they can be released back into their habitat.
  • Habitat Restoration: Efforts to restore natural habitats damaged by fire.

Available Teams

Various teams across regions are available for wildlife rescue, including:

  • Wildlife Rehabilitation Centers
  • Local NGOs focused on animal welfare
  • Volunteer Groups and Citizen Firefighters
  • Government Wildlife Services
